User Experience (UX) Design: Web designers focus on creating a positive user experience by organizing and structuring the website's content in a user-friendly manner. They consider factors such as ease of navigation, intuitive interface design, and accessibility to ensure that users can easily find information and interact with the website. Front-End Development: While web designers primarily focus on the visual aspects, many are also proficient in front-end development languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
